About Tim:

Tim is a 27yrs old engineer who loves to sing and to play his guitar in his spare time!
Currently (02/02/2021) he's contesting in the Belgian version of "The Voice".
And in the past he has contested on the popular Belgian Studio Brussel.

Planned Gig's

Due to Covid-19 there are no concrete plans for futured gig's. We will keep you posted here and on social-media.
